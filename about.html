Our company history

How it all began

Our company was established in 2012, at Lomé, Togo. We are specialised in the creation of tailor-made suits and garments and offer you the best quality of fabrics.

It is our aim to combine traditional African clothing and modern tendency so that our creations can be used in any kind of occasion, in and outside of Africa, be it at a wedding, a business meeting, an anniversary celebration, etc.

The talent behind AfriFashion

Being the fourth generation of fashion designers in her family, Lilas learnt the profession from her mother at a studio in the suburbs of Lomé. She started her own bespoken tailoring house in 2008, at the tender age of 19. With a degree in Communication Design, she has managed to establish a solid base of Togolese clients, who are loyal to her signature blend of sensuality and tradition. Influential chef, Fafa Gilbert is one of those clients, whom she dressed for our Bold Romantics campaign.

What we offer you

We use an individual pattern for every customer. Our tailors handpick all fabrics – from a quality and fashion point of view – and we let you choose from a wide variety of quality fabrics in different categories, prices and qualities. From the most exclusive to the very affordable – all our fabrics are of very good and exclusive quality.

AfriFashion guarantees a handcrafted process of cutting, sewing, trimming and pressing your exclusive tailormade suits, dresses, skirts and shirts.
